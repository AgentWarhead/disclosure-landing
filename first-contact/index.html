// CIVILIAN FIRST CONTACT PROTOCOL //

The first minute has rules.

Empowering beats panic. The visitor should leave knowing there is a sequence, and that sequence starts with them.

01

Stabilize the room

Breathe. Stop sudden movement. Keep the group from rushing forward. Panic spreads faster than light in a crowd.

02

Create distance

Do not approach the object or presence. Establish a buffer, reduce noise, and move children or dependents away.

03

Document cleanly

Record time, location, sound, light, motion, weather, witnesses, and sequence. Do not invent missing details.

04

Assign roles

Protectors hold space. Communicators de-escalate. Observers capture details. Survivors move people out.

05

Do not perform

No shouting, chasing, weapons, lights in the face, or dramatic gestures. First contact is not content. It is a contact zone.

06

Train before it matters

Your nervous system will not rise to a fantasy. It will fall to the protocol you have rehearsed.
