What Is Disclosure?

Disclosure is the world's first civilian first contact preparation platform. It exists because governments have spent decades building classified protocols for extraterrestrial contact — and none of them involve you.

The SETI Post-Detection Protocol. The Pentagon's UAP Task Force. NATO response frameworks. The United Nations has discussed appointing an official first contact representative. Every institution on Earth has a plan for what happens when contact is confirmed.

Civilians have nothing.

Disclosure fills that gap. It uses a 10-question psychological assessment to classify users into one of five archetypes, each with a specific role during a contact event. It then provides archetype-specific training protocols, immersive drills, and a digital First Contact Card — a verifiable credential for Apple and Google Wallet.

The question was never whether contact would happen. The question was always: who speaks for Earth when it does, and what do the rest of us do?
