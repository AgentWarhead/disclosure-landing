What Is UAP Readiness?

UAP readiness is the measure of how prepared a civilian is to respond to an unidentified anomalous phenomena encounter — in real time, without support, with no protocol in hand.

It is not about whether you believe. It is not about your knowledge of government programs or declassified files. It is about one thing: what you do in the sixty seconds after contact occurs, when there is no one to call and no manual to open.

Most people have a readiness score below 30. Not because they are unintelligent or unprepared in other areas of life. Because this is a scenario that has never had civilian training infrastructure — until now.

The Readiness Score

Your Readiness Score is generated by the Disclosure archetype assessment and reflected on your First Contact Card. It measures four axes: composure under extreme-unknown conditions, instinctive role orientation, protocol retention capacity, and situational awareness response time.

It is not a grade. It is a baseline. And every training module in the Disclosure app is designed to move it upward.
