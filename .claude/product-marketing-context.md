## Product Overview
**One-liner:** The world's first civilian first contact preparation app — part ARG, part survival tool, 100% viral.

**Product name:** DISCLOSURE
**URL:** getdisclosure.app
**Product type:** Mobile app (iOS + Android) with landing page funnel
**Business model:** Free app with premium features (freemium). Landing page drives app installs.
**Stage:** Pre-launch (landing page live, app in development)

## Target Audience
**Demographics:** Ages 18-45, predominantly male (60/40), tech-savvy, culturally online
**Psychographics:** UAP/UFO curious (not necessarily believers), conspiracy-adjacent but mainstream-leaning, loves immersive experiences (ARGs, escape rooms, interactive fiction), shares interesting content on social media
**Primary use case:** "I saw the UAP hearings / Trump's executive order and want to know what I'd actually do if contact happened"
**Jobs to be done:**
1. Satisfy curiosity about their "type" in a first contact scenario (identity/classification)
2. Feel prepared and in-the-know about something most people aren't thinking about
3. Get a shareable digital credential/card that signals they're "classified"

## Personas
- **The Believer:** Already follows UAP news. Wants validation and community. Shares everything.
- **The Skeptic-Curious:** Doesn't believe but finds the premise fascinating. Drawn by the ARG/immersive quality.
- **The Content Creator:** Sees viral potential. Will take the quiz for content. Shares archetype card on Stories/TikTok.
- **The Doomscroller:** Encounters it via share. Takes quiz impulsively. May or may not stick around.

## The Four Archetypes
- 🛡️ **Sentinel** (red #EF4444, ~34%) — Primary Protector. "I secure the perimeter."
- 🤝 **Diplomat** (green #22C55E, ~41%) — De-escalation Lead. "I make first contact peaceful."
- 🔬 **Scholar** (blue #60A5FA, ~23%) — Field Analyst. "I document everything."
- ⭐ **First Contact** (gold #FFD700, <0.1%) — Hidden archetype. Outside standard classification. Secret unlock sequence.

## Problems & Pain Points
- Real UAP disclosure is happening (congressional hearings, executive orders) but zero civilian preparation exists
- People feel powerless/uninformed about something governments have spent decades studying
- No framework for "what would I actually do?" — it's all abstract
- Existing UAP content is either dry government docs or conspiracy theory noise — nothing immersive and actionable

## Competitive Landscape
- **Direct competitors:** None. No app does this.
- **Secondary competitors:** BuzzFeed-style personality quizzes (shallow, no depth or lore), UAP news apps (informational, not experiential)
- **Indirect competitors:** ARGs, immersive theater, escape room apps (different topic, same engagement model)

## Differentiation
- Only app that treats first contact preparation as a real, credentialed experience
- Archetype system creates identity and belonging (not just a quiz result — it's *who you are*)
- Grounded in real UAP timeline events (congressional hearings, executive orders — all verifiable)
- First Contact Card = Apple/Google Wallet digital credential = viral sharing mechanism
- 12 confirmed features spanning training, assessment, communication tools, and community

## Objections
1. "This isn't real" → We know. That's the point. It's preparation theater — like a fire drill. You don't need a real fire to benefit from knowing what to do.
2. "UFO stuff is for conspiracy theorists" → The US Congress held hearings. A president signed an executive order. 40+ witnesses testified under oath. This is mainstream now.
3. "It's just a personality quiz" → The quiz is the entry point. The app has 12 features including signal transmission tools, stone cold drills, family drill mode, and a global liaison map.
