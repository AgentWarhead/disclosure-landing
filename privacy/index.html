// 01. WHAT WE COLLECT //

Only what the systemneeds to function.

Depending on how you use Disclosure, we may collect or process account identifiers, email address if provided, archetype designation, readiness score, clearance level, training progress, app settings, purchase entitlement status, crash diagnostics, and basic usage analytics.

  • Classification data Archetype, readiness score, clearance level, and training state.
  • Account data Email or login identifier if the app account system requires it.
  • Purchase data Entitlement confirmation from Apple, Google, or RevenueCat. We do not receive full card numbers.
  • Diagnostics Crash reports and aggregate usage signals used to keep the app working.
// 02. WHAT WE DO NOT WANT //

Some data is useless.Some data is liability.

Disclosure does not need your real-world identity to be useful. We do not design the app around harvesting location trails, contact lists, private messages, biometrics, or payment-card details.

  • No sale We do not sell your personal information.
  • No contact scraping We do not need your address book.
  • No payment card storage App store payment systems handle billing.
  • No unnecessary permissions Device permissions should map to visible product features.
// 03. HOW WE USE DATA //

The purpose is narrow.Keep it that way.

We use data to provide the app, save your progress, issue or validate features, process purchases, improve reliability, respond to support requests, prevent abuse, and comply with applicable law.

We do not use your classification data to make real-world eligibility, employment, credit, housing, insurance, or government decisions. It is a product designation inside Disclosure.
// 04. THIRD PARTIES //

Processors are named.No mystery pipes.

Disclosure may use trusted service providers for hosting, authentication, purchase validation, analytics, diagnostics, and app infrastructure. Current or planned providers may include Supabase, RevenueCat, Expo, Apple App Store, Google Play, and analytics or crash-reporting services required to operate the app.

These providers process data for service operation. They do not receive permission from us to sell your personal data.

// 05. RETENTION AND DELETION //

Your file should not live forever.Neither should ours.

We keep account and progress data while your account is active or while needed to provide the service. Purchase and legal records may be retained as required for accounting, fraud prevention, app store obligations, dispute resolution, or law.

You can request access, correction, export, or deletion by contacting team@getdisclosure.app. Some backups or required records may take time to expire, but we will process reasonable requests as required by applicable law.

// 06. SECURITY AND CHILDREN //

Secure by default.Not childish by design.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect data. No system is perfectly secure, but minimizing data reduces the blast radius if anything ever goes wrong.

Disclosure is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will review deletion.
